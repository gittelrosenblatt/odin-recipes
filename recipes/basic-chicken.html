Basic Oven-Baked Chicken

Description

This recipe will teach you how to make simple oven-cooked chicken. It'll take about 30 minutes to cook at 375 degrees Fahrenheit.

  • 1 pound boneless, skinless chicken
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp black pepper
  • 1/2 tsp garlic powder
  • 1/2 tsp paprika (optional, for color)
  • 1/2 tsp chili powder or Italian seasoning (optional, for flavor)

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C).
  2. Pat chicken dry with paper towels. Place in a baking dish or a foil-lined baking sheet.
  3. Rub chicken with olive oil, then sprinkle both sides with salt, pepper, garlic powder, and any other spices you want.
  4. Bake for 30 minutes, or until internal temperature reaches 165 degrees F (74 degrees C).
  5. Let the chicken rest for 5 minutes before shredding or slicing.

And there you have it! You just made easy chicken in the oven. If you're looking for a recipe to use it with, check out our Chicken Sandwich recipe.
