Chicken Sandwich

Description

This recipe will teach you how to make a chicken sandwich using bread of your choice. The toppings are lettuce, cheese, pickles, and homemade garlic sauce.

The chicken sandwich assembly will take about 10-15 minutes and make 2 sandwiches, but you need to already have cooked chicken. If you don't, visit our Basic Oven-Baked Chicken recipe.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound cooked chicken, shredded or sliced
  • 1 medium tomato, sliced
  • 1/2 cup shredded lettuce
  • cheese of your choice, 2 slices
  • 1/4 cup of butter
  • 3 garlic cloves, peeled and minced
  • bread or roll of your choice
