Chicken Sandwich

Description

This recipe will teach you how to make a chicken sandwich using bread of your choice. The toppings are lettuce, cheese, pickles, and homemade garlic sauce. The chicken sandwich assembly will take about 10-15 minutes and make 2 sandwiches, but you need to already have cooked chicken. If you don't, visit our Basic Oven-Baked Chicken recipe.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound cooked chicken, shredded or sliced
  • 1 medium tomato, sliced
  • 1/2 cup shredded lettuce
  • 3/4 cup cheese of your choice
  • 1/4 cup of butter
  • 3 garlic cloves, peeled and minced
  • roll of your choice

Steps

  1. Turn stove onto medium-high heat.
  2. Melt butter, garlic, and cheese together for five minutes, stirring constantly.
  3. Pour or spread garlic-cheese mixture on the bottom side of each roll.
  4. Divide chicken between the roll bottoms.
  5. Divide lettuce and tomato and place on top of chicken.
  6. Close sandwich and enjoy!

Congratulations, you successfully made a yummy chicken sandwich! Now enjoy it!
